Overview

The cognitive architecture for Bob's Discount Furniture AI agent is designed to enhance the customer experience by structuring interactions around a well-defined hierarchy. The care group comprises two primary teams—Sales and Service—supported by an AI agent that serves as a concierge and automation assistant. The AI is empowered to address user inquiries, streamline processes, and ensure seamless interactions across various customer journeys. This architecture integrates the primary groups, user stages, topics, guides, tools, and measures to create a unified, efficient, and collaborative care function across digital messaging and voice channels.

Primary Groups

The AI first classifies inquiries into one of two primary groups: Sales or Service. Sales inquiries encompass requests for assistance with purchasing new furniture, understanding promotions, and customizing orders. Service inquiries cover post-purchase needs such as delivery status, rescheduling, warranty claims, or product issues. By distinguishing between these two groups, the AI ensures that the inquiry is routed to the appropriate team or handled autonomously where applicable. This differentiation allows the system to address user needs more effectively while aligning with the organizational structure.

Journey Stages

The AI further refines its understanding of the inquiry by identifying the user's stage in the Customer Journey. These stages include:

  • Pre-Purchase: Customers exploring product details, availability, or promotions.
  • Pre-Delivery Scheduling: Customers who have purchased and need to arrange delivery.
  • Delivery Scheduled: Customers confirming or preparing for their scheduled delivery.
  • Day of Delivery: Real-time coordination and tracking on the delivery day.
  • Recently Delivered: Assistance with immediate post-delivery needs such as assembly or damages.
  • Furniture in the Home: Long-term inquiries about care, warranty, or recycling.

Understanding the user stage enables the AI to provide tailored responses and proactively address potential concerns.

Topics

The AI categorizes customer inquiries into key topics to streamline responses and provide targeted assistance. These topics include:

  • Product Information Requests: General questions about furniture specifications such as dimensions, materials, and care instructions.
  • Order Placement Assistance: Specific requests for help with placing an order through the website or in-store.
  • Order, Delivery and Service Status Updates: Customers checking the status of their order, including shipping and delivery timelines.
  • Delivery Scheduling: Assistance with scheduling or rescheduling delivery dates and understanding delivery options.
  • Order Modifications: Customer requests for modifying existing orders, such as changing color, size, or quantity.
  • Product Availability Checks: Inquiries about the availability of specific items, including wait times for out-of-stock products.
  • Return and Exchange Inquiries: Assist customers with the process for returning or exchanging items, and understanding associated policies.
  • Warranty Service Requests: Questions about warranty terms and requests for service under warranty conditions.
  • Refund Processes: Inquiries on how to request refunds for returned items or orders canceled by the customer or due to inventory issues.
  • Assembly Instructions: Questions about assembly of furniture items, availability of assembly services, and troubleshooting assembly problems.
  • Promotions and Discounts: Information about current promotions, sales events, and eligibility for discounts.
  • Payment and Billing Issues: Help with issues related to payments, billing errors, or financing options.
  • Website Navigation Help: Assistance with navigating the online store, using features like wish lists and product comparisons.
  • Customer Feedback and Complaints: Providing a channel for customer feedback or filing complaints about their shopping experience.
  • Store Pickup Inquiries: Help with managing store pickups.

Organizing inquiries by topic ensures the AI delivers precise and efficient solutions tailored to customer needs.

Guides

The AI uses guided processes to handle specific scenarios efficiently, ensuring customers receive consistent and accurate support. These guides include:

  • Sales Escalation: Handles inquiries that require escalation to the sales team for assistance with product recommendations, promotions, and order placement.
  • Day of Delivery: Provides real-time support for customers on their delivery day, including tracking updates and coordination with delivery teams.
  • Delivery Scheduling: Assists customers in scheduling or rescheduling furniture deliveries, offering available date options and confirming details.
  • Recently Delivered: Supports customers with post-delivery needs, such as resolving immediate issues with assembly, damages, or missing items.
  • Store Pickup: Guides customers through the process of scheduling and managing store pickup for their furniture orders.
  • Product Issues: Helps customers report and resolve issues with purchased products, including warranty claims, damages, and defects.
  • General Support: Provides assistance with a variety of general inquiries and concerns, ensuring comprehensive support for all customer needs.

Tools

The AI agent utilizes various tools to automate processes and enhance customer service. These tools include:

  • Get Order Status: Provide customers with real-time updates on their order status, including processing, shipping, and delivery timelines.
  • Confirm Delivery: Allow customers to confirm their scheduled delivery details, such as date, time, and address, ensuring all arrangements are correct.
  • Schedule Delivery: Help customers schedule the delivery of their furniture by selecting available dates and providing confirmation.
  • Reschedule Delivery: Assist customers in rescheduling their delivery by providing new options for dates and updating delivery details.
  • Day of Delivery Tracking: Provide real-time updates and tracking information for deliveries happening on the scheduled day.
  • Report a Product Issue: Allow customers to report issues with their delivered products, including damages or missing items, and initiate support for resolution.

Measure

To evaluate the effectiveness of the AI agent, we rely on the following key performance measurements:

  • Response Accuracy: The percentage of responses correctly aligned with customer inquiries, ensuring accurate and helpful information is provided.
  • Resolution Rate: The proportion of customer inquiries successfully resolved by the AI without the need for escalation to a human agent.
  • Customer Satisfaction (CSAT): Feedback collected from customers after interactions, measuring their satisfaction with the AI's assistance.
  • Use Case Goals:Goal achievement related to guides objectives and tool automations.

These measurements help ensure the AI agent continuously improves and delivers exceptional customer service experiences.
